# Basim general business + AI Threads notes

Session-derived guidance for adapting Basim's Threads content to a broader, less technical audience.

## Audience

- Pemula yang baru mulai usaha and still doing everything alone.
- Owners with several businesses where all decisions, reports, and small problems still return to them.
- Businesses receiving many chats/leads but losing customers because follow-up and records are messy.
- People curious about AI but confused where to start and intimidated by tools/setup.

## Language level

Use plain Indonesian business language. Avoid technical-first framing.

Prefer:

- alat bantu AI
- sistem kecil
- kerjaan berulang
- catatan order/customer
- follow up
- rekap harian
- laporan harian
- balasan customer
- chat kelewat
- tim nggak perlu nunggu owner

Avoid unless user explicitly wants technical content:

- API
- server
- terminal
- extension
- OpenClaw/Hermes-specific jargon
- product engineering
- MVP
- scope/requirements

## Strong thread angles

1. **Baru mulai usaha, tapi sudah kewalahan**
   - Hook: person starts business enthusiastically but does posting, chat, orders, payment checks, shipping alone.
   - Twist: not only lack of customers; all work is stacked on one person.
   - AI angle: draft replies, order recap, follow-up reminders, chat summaries.

2. **Owner punya banyak bisnis, tapi semua balik ke dia**
   - Hook: outside looks successful, but every report/decision/problem waits for owner.
   - Twist: not always an incapable team; workflow and records are unclear.
   - AI angle: summarize reports, remind follow-ups, surface priorities.

3. **Chat ramai, customer hilang**
   - Hook: many inquiries but sales do not rise much.
   - Twist: marketing may be working; customers leak during chat/follow-up.
   - AI angle: label customer status, remind follow-up, summarize conversations.

4. **Mau pakai AI, bingung mulai dari mana**
   - Hook: owner hears AI can help everything but gets stuck choosing tools.
   - Twist: question is too big; start from repetitive daily work.
   - AI angle: customer replies, captions, daily reports, follow-up lists, chat summaries.

5. **Bisnis ramai, sistem belum ikut naik**
   - Hook: order/chat/team/customer demand grows; old manual habits crack.
   - Twist: not business failure; operating system has not grown with the business.
   - AI angle: rekap order, reminder, templates, report summaries, team notifications.

## Structure for broad-audience threads

Use 4 posts when possible:

1. Human story + loss/tension + twist.
2. Explain the simple hidden mechanism.
3. List concrete everyday symptoms.
4. Practical AI/automation help + light insight/question.

Keep every post under 500 characters for Threads/Repliz scheduling. Count characters with a tool before presenting or scheduling.
